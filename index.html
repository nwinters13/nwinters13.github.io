Nathan Winters
*
Born and raised in Dover, New Hampshire, I fell in love with the problem-solving nature of programming during my freshman year at Tufts. After taking the beginnning courses and diving into web programming and machine structure, I spent Summer 2014 at Intersystems, a well-established data management and analytics software company in Cambridge, MA. There I received a great experience, diving into a new language while still working with HTML/CSS and JavaScript. I currently hold a research position with the Visual Analytics Lab at Tufts, researching and programming a tool to help the recently diagnosed decide best treatment options. When I'm not thinking about bits and bytes, I can be found playing intramural sports, doing the Tufts Dance Collective, or perhaps playing some video games. I'm excited to delve deeper into the world of Computer Science and hope to gain more rewarding experiences that were as rewarding as my last.
